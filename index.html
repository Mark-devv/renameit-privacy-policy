ReNameIt — Privacy Policy

Last Updated: August 9, 2025

ReNameIt is a Chrome extension designed to let you rename text on any webpage. We take your privacy seriously. This extension does not collect, store, or share any personal information.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any data. All renaming actions and settings are stored locally in your browser using Chrome’s storage system and are never sent to any external server.

How Your Data is Used

All changes you make remain on your device. There is no tracking, analytics, or remote storage involved.

Third Parties

We do not sell, share, or transfer any user data to third parties. No remote code or third-party scripts are executed.
